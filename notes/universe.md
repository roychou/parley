# Investment Universe

- [TSLA] | Tesla | Energy, Robotics, Automobiles
  - In 2026, the thesis has shifted from just "selling cars" to AI Robotics. Investors buy Tesla for the potential of FSD (Full Self-Driving), the "Optimus" humanoid robot, and its massive Energy Storage business.

- [GOOGL] | Google | Frontier AI, AI Infra, Cloud Infra
  - "Gemini" ecosystem and its $80B+ cloud run rate make it a powerhouse in both consumer and business AI.

- [AMZN] | Amazon | Cloud Infra, Internet Provider
  - Beyond retail, the investment case is built on AWS (Cloud) and the new "Amazon Leo" (formerly Project Kuiper) satellite internet.

- [INTC] | Intel | AI Semiconductor
  - Turnaround play for investors betting on its "foundry" model—the Western world's primary alternative to TSMC.

- [NVDA] | Nvidia | AI Semiconductor
  - Dominant king of AI training chips (Blackwell and Rubin architectures).

- [MSFT] | Microsoft Corporation | Technology
  - AI Software & Cloud leaders. Microsoft has a massive lead in enterprise AI (Copilot).

- [GEV] | GE Vernova | Industrials Power Generation
  - Essential for grid upgrades and powering AI data centers.

- [FIX] | Comfort Systems USA | Industrials
  - Designs and maintains critical systems like heaters, air conditioners, and electrical wiring for commercial clients.

- [MOG-A] | Moog Inc. | Industrials
  - Precision motion and fluid controls for aerospace, defense, and industrial applications.

- [EME] | EMCOR Group | Industrials
  - Mechanical and electrical construction and facilities services for hospitals and data centers.

- [EMBJ] | Embraer | Industrials
  - Aerospace and defense company focusing on commercial/executive aircraft and eVTOL technologies.

- [PRYMY] | Prysmian S.p.A | Industrials
  - Produces and distributes cables and systems for energy and telecommunications worldwide.

- [VRTX] | Vertex Pharmaceuticals | Healthcare
  - Global leader in cystic fibrosis treatments with expansion into gene editing and pain management.

- [ATZAF] | Aritzia Inc | Consumer Cyclical
  - "Everyday Luxury" apparel design and retail expansion across North America.

- [RKLB] | Rocket Lab USA | Industrials
  - End-to-end space company building rockets and satellite components.
